What is Online Marketing?

Online marketing is the promotion of products and services through the internet. It includes social media marketing, email marketing, SEO, and online advertising.

Types of Online Marketing

  • Social Media Marketing
  • Email Marketing
  • SEO (Search Engine Optimization)
  • Content Marketing
  • PPC Advertising

Benefits

  • Global reach
  • Low cost
  • 24/7 promotion
  • Easy to measure results
  • Brand awareness
